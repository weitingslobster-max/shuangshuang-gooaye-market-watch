Theme analysis

1. TSMC: anchor status unchanged, proof still pending

EP675 made TSMC the near-term anchor after the 6/30 rebalance window. Today’s state-only review does not add new Gooaye input and does not change the thesis. The next useful proof remains official TSMC monthly revenue and the Q2 earnings call: AI/HPC demand, gross margin, overseas fab cost, capex discipline and market reaction.

Stance: 核心觀察 / wait for better entry. Upgrade only if official results and guidance beat high expectations and the stock confirms with constructive price/volume behavior after the event.

2. Passive components: alive, but still not proven enough

The passive/R-chip/MLCC thesis remains active because book-to-bill, backlog and price-letter anecdotes are consistent with a possible upcycle. Vishay’s official Q1 release reported revenue of $839.2 million, gross margin of 21.0%, book-to-bill of 1.34, passive-component book-to-bill of 1.23 and 5.7 months of backlog. That is supportive, but it is not yet enough to upgrade the group.

Stance: 優先研究 / evidence-gated. Require price-letter confirmation plus revenue, gross-margin and cash-flow conversion. VSH is liquid but imperfect; MRAAY is thematically closer but OTC-liquidity constrained.

3. Power / MOSFET / mature-node: plausible capacity externality, not a confirmed shortage trade

The EP675 hypothesis remains plausible: high-end AI demand may absorb IDM priority capacity and create second-source demand in lower-tier power and mature-node lanes. However, a tradeable upgrade requires more than plausible externality. We need official product-mix, lead-time, utilization and margin proof from ON, STM, TXN, UMC and MPWR.

Stance: 優先研究 / no chase. Keep the dashboard active; do not promote on channel rumor alone.

4. ON + SYNA: Physical-AI story is real, but deal risk is also real

The official onsemi/Synaptics announcement describes an all-stock transaction with about $7 billion enterprise value, a fixed exchange ratio of 1.350 ON shares per SYNA share, and a strategic push toward Physical AI across power, sense, connected compute and control. This is directly relevant to the EP675 lane.

The investment conclusion is still cautious: all-stock M&A creates dilution and integration risk, and it does not automatically validate the underlying auto/industrial cycle. SYNA remains an event monitor; ON remains priority research.

5. IC design and AI application layer: use US names only when direct evidence exists

Taiwan IC-design names remain useful industry signals, but the US-tradable expressions must be judged on their own evidence. QCOM, AVGO, MRVL, NXPI, GOOGL, MSFT, AAPL and MU should not be bought merely because local signals move. The necessary proof is direct revenue, margin, customer breadth, capex ROI or HBM/device-demand conversion.
