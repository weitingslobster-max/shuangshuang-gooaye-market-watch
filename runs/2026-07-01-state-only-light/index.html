  • GOOGL remains a priority platform monitor. No new completed Gooaye input changed the thesis today. The hard gate is still Cloud growth, Gemini/search monetization, ad durability, margin, and capex-return proof from Alphabet IR.
  • AAPL remains watch-only for device/edge-AI pricing power. Product announcements alone do not upgrade the stance; the needed evidence is device demand, mix, gross margin, and whether AI features support upgrade cycles without discounting.

MU/HBM and hyperscaler capex ROI: still priority, still cyclical

Micron remains a high-signal HBM/data-center memory monitor from the current state. The key discipline is to pair supplier strength with hyperscaler ROI: HBM strength can help MU while raising costs for MSFT/GOOGL/META/AMZN. No state upgrade without inventory, pricing, margin, and customer capex-efficiency proof.

AVGO/MRVL custom silicon and networking: evidence strong, but no chase

  • AVGO remains priority research. Broadcom's Q2 FY2026 release reported record revenue/operating profit/free cash flow and accelerating AI semiconductor revenue; it guided Q3 FY2026 revenue to about $29.4B. This supports the custom AI ASIC/networking lane, but ABF/Whitefish package-intensity details still need official or high-quality confirmation before converting into substrate-stock conclusions.
  • MRVL remains a priority but high-beta monitor. Marvell's Q1 FY2027 release reported net revenue of $2.418B, up 28% year over year, and guided Q2 revenue to a $2.7B midpoint. This reinforces AI/custom/optical demand, but customer concentration, margin durability, and valuation risk remain live.

Passive, power and mature-node: no upgrade without conversion

The passive/R-chip, AI power-tree and mature-node lanes remain proof-gated:

  • VSH is still the liquid US proxy for passive/discrete conversion, but needs book-to-bill, pricing, margin and FCF proof.
  • MRAAY/MRAAF stay signal/small-satellite only because OTC liquidity and spread risk matter.
  • MPWR/ON/STM/TXN/UMC/ADI need product-specific AI power, PMIC, MOSFET or utilization evidence. Generic semiconductor recovery wording is not enough.
